muscle model card game

*** is a game for exploring muscle contraction in the upper and lower extremities. Use the model to demonstrate contractions and work together to answer questions for points. Collect as many points as you can in 15 minutes!

HOW TO PLAY

Read these instructions out loud to the whole team:

Everyone in your group is on the same team but there are two roles in this game. YOu will each take turns being the question master and picking up a card. On your turn...

  1. Pick up a card and DO NOT show your card to your team!
  2. Set up the model in the START POSITION shown on the card
  3. Once the model is set up, say “movement start”
  4. Use the syringes to show the muscle contraction shown in the SHOW MOVEMENT part of the card
  5. Read the questions on the card out loud - your team will work together to answer the questions
  6. Check their answers at the bottom of the card, or find hints on this website
  7. If any question is answered incorrectly, put the card in the 'TRY AGAIN' pile and review answers online.
  8. If ALL questions are answered correctly, put the card on the 'YOU WIN' pile.

When the time runs out…

  1. Add up your points (written on the back of the cards)
  2. Tell a TA how many points your team got
  3. Reset the game
