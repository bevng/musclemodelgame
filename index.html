muscle model card game

Gameplay instructions...

Your team is working together to answer questions about muscle contractions and movements. You will take turns picking a card and using the muscle model to demonstrate the movements. The rest of your team will answer questions based on your demonstration. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins!

On your turn…

  1. Pick up a card and DO NOT show your card to the rest of your team!
  2. Use the card image and description to set up the muscle model in the initial position of the movement
  3. Say “movement start”
  4. Use the syringes to show the muscle contraction/movement
  5. Ask the rest of the team the questions on the card and check their answers at the bottom of the card
  6. If any question is answered incorrectly, look on the site to review your knowledge and try again.
  7. Once ALL questions are answered correctly, put the card on the 'YOU WIN' pile.

When the time runs out…

  1. Pick up all the 'YOU WIN' cards and add up your points
  2. Tell a TA how many points your team got
  3. Put all played cards back into their respective Level piles
