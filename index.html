muscle model card game

Gameplay instructions (Read these instructions out loud to the whole team)

Your team is working together to answer questions about muscle contractions and movements. You will take turns picking a card and using the muscle model to demonstrate the movements. The rest of your team will answer questions based on your demonstration. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins!

On your turn…

  1. Pick up a card
  2. Don’t show anyone else your card!
  3. Use the card image and description to set up the muscle model to show the initial position of the movement
  4. Say “movement start”
  5. Use the syringes to show the muscle contraction/movement
  6. Ask the rest of the team the questions on the card.
  7. Check the answer at the bottom of the card
  8. If any question is answered incorrectly, put the card in the TRY AGAIN pile
  9. If all questions are answered correctly, put the card in the WINNER pile

When the time runs out…

  1. Pick up all the WINNER cards and add up your points
  2. Tell a TA how many points your team got
  3. Put all played cards back into their respective Level piles
